__What are you building and why?__

We’re building NeuroCog LMS, a cognitive\-aware AI recommendation engine that plugs into existing LMS platforms instead of replacing them\. It uses multimodal discovery intake, including surveys, video, handwriting, audio chat, and assessments, to build cognitive and motivational learner profiles, then recommends three personalized learning paths with confidence scores and plain\-language rationale\. We’re building it because most LMS platforms treat learners as interchangeable\. NeuroCog helps learners, educators, and institutions adapt learning paths to how someone actually thinks, not just what course they are enrolled in\. 

__why\_you  
__ We’re the right team because we combine lived understanding of neurodivergent learning, edtech founder experience, career\-transition expertise, product architecture, and full\-stack AI implementation\. We are building cognitive\-aware discovery and pathway recommendation before course placement\.
